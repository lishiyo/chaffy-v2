chaffy

chaffy is the anonymous
mobile chat app

We sprung out of Talk2, a simple Firebase + Angular project for our awesome NYC bootcamp, App Factory.

Our hope is to let you hang out with the people around you, whether you're shy, bored, lonely or just addicted to your phone.

There are a lot of chat apps out there, but we think we're a little different. We're all about location - open up a chatroom right at your cafe - and hanging out with total strangers, rather than just your friends network. We're all about anyone, anytime, anywhere - just type in a chat to store it in your dash forever, and search for chats anywhere in the world.

Every chat here is 100% anonymous.
We will never sell your data.
We will never charge you anything.
Help us by forking us on github (lishiyo/chaffy) and sharing your ideas!
